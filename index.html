One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda,
it has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, and has been collected into 98 tankōbon volumes as of April 2021.
The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after unintentionally eating a Devil Fruit.
With his pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates. Luffy explores the Grand Line in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King.
